About us

Welcome to Good & Green - Your Island Fresh Market

Nestled in the lush paradise of Kauai's North Shore, Good & Green brings you nature's finest treasures - fresh coconuts and tropical melons grown in rich volcanic soil under the Hawaiian sun.

Our family farm has been cultivating these delicious fruits for generations, maintaining traditional farming practices while embracing sustainable agriculture. Every coconut and melon we offer is hand-picked at peak ripeness to ensure the freshest, most flavorful experience.
