Meet Engel Perez

Where Music, Movement, and Mother Nature Meet

The Soul Behind the Coconuts

More than just a coconut vendor, Engel Perez is a multifaceted artist whose passion for life flows through everything he does - from cracking open fresh coconuts to creating soulful music.

Artist

  • Singer/Songwriter
  • Music Producer

Performer

  • Bboy/Dancer
  • Teacher

Health & Wellness Journey

Combining his passion for music with a dedication to health and wellness, Engel brings the natural goodness of Kauai's coconuts to people while spreading positive vibes through his art. His journey as a musician and wellness advocate led him to appreciate the simple yet profound benefits of nature's gifts.

Music & Movement

As a singer-songwriter and music producer, Engel creates rhythms that move both body and soul. His background as a Bboy and dancer infuses energy into everything he does - whether he's performing on stage or serving up fresh coconuts.

Follow the Journey

Natural Living

Through Good & Green, Engel shares his love for natural living and wellness. Each coconut comes with a side of positive energy and aloha spirit, making every sip an experience that nourishes both body and soul.
